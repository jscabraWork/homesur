CORPORATE RESPONSABILTY

Business model aligned with
international standards of
sustainability (IRIS+ & GRESB), implementing
best-in-class environmental, social and governance practices.

  • • Proprietary collection of sold waste and recycling policies in all facilities.
  • • Comparative assessment of environmental performance by GRESB standards.

Social

  • • 5% of retail gross leasable area reserved and subsidized for LG.

  • • Ethics, corruption, sustainability and compliance policies.
  • • Full-time sustainability manager to comply with ESG standards.
